Backbone.js Experiments

This site will demonstrate the power of having a backbone.

Backbone Introduction

backbone.js is a small, free JavaScript Model-View-Controller (MVC) framework.

Backbone provides models and views which can be extended and wired together. This provides a easy means of structuring a javascript applications.

Why Experiment

An experiment is a methodical procedure carried out with the goal of verifying, falsifying, or establishing the validity of a hypothesis.

Wikipedia

We are trying to verifying the hypothesis that the backbone.js is a great means of structuring javascript applications.
